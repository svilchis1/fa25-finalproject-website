    Marriage Trends in the U.S. and Demographics

    Marriage has been an important part of all cultures throughout history. For religious or civil reasons, marriage continues to be a binding pact between many people. However, marriage trends has changed throughout the years, and comparing marriage between certain groups allows us to better understand the bigger picture of the nation's relationship with marriage. How have certain demographics responded to the times concerning marriage? Has marriage been declining for all people in the United States? Through data compiled on marriage and divorce trends, we can get a better view on the past, current, and future state of marriage in the United States.
